> *"The past has nothing to do with anything. ......Just as it does not matter the name of the tree from whose trunk a piece of furniture was originally made."*

After what feels like an eternity, **Golden Forgery** is finally out. The first public build of the fanmade *Umineko Golden Fantasia* expansion is ready to be torn apart by the army of goats. 

<!--more-->

## What's new

- New moves for every character in the roster
- Entirely new moveset for Black Battler. He's not just Battler but Black anymore
- Two new color palettes for every character
- Rebalanced framedata and hurtboxes for the existing cast

Full patch notes will be in a follow-up post. Please look forward to it.

## Getting it

Head to the [**Install**](/install/) page for system requirements, the download link, and the step-by-step. The short version: just download the zip file and extract it in the game's root folder.

## Found a bug?

Let us know in [the Discord!](https://discord.gg/NSGHEymyjM)! We'll be glad to help.

## What's next

While this is a huge milestone for the team, we plan to continue updating the game with balance patches and new features. We can't give any timelines as this is essentially voluntary work for everyone involved, but please check back from time to time for more news.

## Thanks

To the team, what we've done so far is so much more than what I could possibly expect back in 2023, when I just wanted to fix the framerate bug. You've given me everything, and I'll spend the rest of my life repaying this debt.

I cannot express enough gratitude to everyone who kept playing *Umineko Golden Fantasia* throughout the years (yes, even you). Without your passion, none of this would've been possible.
